Web Design

Forsberg & Evans Law Offices

Kim was the head WordPress developer for the recreation of Forsberg & Evans Law Offices website. She used their old website as a guide and built the site using WordPress and Elementor to improve it.

Potato Products of Idaho

Kim was the lead designer and developer of the redesign of Potato Products of Idaho’s main website. She had consistent communication with her client so she was able to follow through with their directions and requests. Kim designed the website in Figma then made it using Wordpress and Elementor.

S&R Dirtworks

Kim was the head WordPress developer of S&R Dirtworks website. Kim followed the design provided by the client and built the site using WordPress and Elementor.

Sand Creek Ranch

Kim was one of two developers of WordPress recreation of Sand Creek Ranch website. She used their old website as a guide and built the site using WordPress and Elementor.

Idaho Rock and Sand

Kim was one of two WordPress developers of the Idaho Rock and Sand website. Kim followed the design provided by the client and built the site using WordPress and Elementor.

Spunky Monkey Preschool

Kim is the head manager of the Spunky Monkey Preschool website. She has applied updates and changes whenever the client contacts her using HTML and CSS.

Graphic Design

“You are What You Eat” Stickers

Kim came up and put together these funny sticker concepts. She made more than 15 brainstormed sketches on paper then put together the 3 best stickers using Adobe Illustrator.

Wild Cat Icons

Kim created these 20 wild cat icons. She looked at many references of wild cats and simplified them into these icon designs by first sketching them on paper then turning those sketches into digital art using Adobe Illustrator.

About

Kim studied at Brigham Young University - Idaho and graduated with a Bachelor degree in Web Design and Development. She gained HTML and CSS skills in her Web Design classes while learning how to use WordPress during her internship at Binary Cocoa.
